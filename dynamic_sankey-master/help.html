1. Start by giving your Sankey Diagram a name.

2. Next, you can choose a background to have on your Sankey Diagram.

3. Afterwards, you can choose what type of Sankey you prefer to have: Nodes or Arrows.

4. Finally, you can choose whether you wish to have color gradient or not.

5. You can also make your graph more appealing by adding Measurements and/or Primary Units.

6. The next section will be defining your Source and Destination Nodes.

You can then choose a name, value, units, and converted units.

Hit the "Add Source and Destination" button to create as many nodes as needed.

Clicking the bottom bar will allow you to change your node to any color.

7. Once you have finished creating your nodes you can add links

You can then choose the source, destination, value, units, and the converted units.

Create more links by clicking the "Add Source and Destination Link" button.

8. Once you have completed the last step of adding your links you can then

render your Sankey Diagram by pressing the "Render Sankey" button.

9. Once you have your Sankey rendered you can press the "Download Sankey" button,

which allows you to download a picture of your Sankey anywhere on your computer.
